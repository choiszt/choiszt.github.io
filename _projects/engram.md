Engram is an end-to-end encrypted memory system designed for AI assistants — "Signal for AI Memory." Data resides on your device by default with no mandatory cloud synchronization.

### Engram — [Engram](https://github.com/EvolvingLMMs-Lab/engram)
- Local-first architecture with SQLite stored in `~/.engram/`
- AES-256-GCM encryption, PBKDF2-SHA256 key derivation, HMAC-SHA256 blind indexing
- 24-word BIP39 mnemonic recovery phrase
- Zero-knowledge optional multi-device sync
- Compatible with Claude Desktop, Claude Code, Cursor, and any MCP-compatible AI client

### Engram Teams
- Lifecycle hooks for team-based AI workflows
- Automatic consolidation of team memories into categorized summaries
- Tagged retrieval across findings, decisions, hypotheses, blockers, and action items
